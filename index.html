About me

Hey! I'm Nat, a passionate 2d artist from Ukraine.
I have 2 years freelancing experience.
Over this time I have created board
game illustrations, illustrated successful
Kickstarter project "Sapience" and painted
interiors for two Big Bad booklets for HitPointPress!

Traveling has always been my passion
as seeing the world's beauty greatly
inspires me and helps deliver
the best possible environment
for your projects! Also, horse riding
is my true passion, so if you want your
beloved equine's portrait - let me know!
